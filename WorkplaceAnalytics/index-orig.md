# Workplace Analytics

**Workplace Analytics** helps you understand how your organization invests its time with insights into how groups collaborate across the organization. Workplace Analytics quantifies Office 365 collaboration to give business decision makers a powerful tool for evidence-based cultural transformation. [Dashboards](../use/home-page.md) highlight potential problem areas through guided exploration, while [custom queries](../tutorials/query-basics.md) offer flexible data access to create valuable analysis. By augmenting Office 365 data with business outcome data, practitioners can identify best practices, develop predictive models, and establish organizational benchmarks. With add-on consulting services, Workplace Analytics solutions experts will help you use this data to identify business opportunities, implement change, and measure success.
